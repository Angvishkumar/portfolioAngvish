A little about myself ..

My name is Angvish kumar shaw. I'm a web developer at rtcamp. I was born in Jamshedpur, located in Jharkhand. I've been a web developer designer since 2007. I started to develop websites professionally from 2013 with php wordpress based structure. Gradually, I began to use HTML5 and CSS3 to design websites more responsive and user friendly. So I worked on rtcamp from July 2013 to achieve the high ability of developing which I needed. The professional experience in developing, making fresh and creative ideas and advanced web programming made us dependable team for customers. All in all I love developing and it shows in my works.

A quick glimps of my work

The crossover between design and programming has always been of interest to me, and since moving to Pune six months ago, I've been lucky enough to work alongside some talented teams on a number of high profile websites.

I have a wide range of skills that include design (and working closely with designers), front-end development (HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, Responsive, UX), back-end development using open source technologies, Linux server system administration, deployment and performance tuning (Apache, MySQL and NGinx).
